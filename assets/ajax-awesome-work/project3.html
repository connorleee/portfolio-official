Friend Finder
by Connor
User is initially asked for their name and a picture of themselves that will be saved in a database along with the values of their responses to 10 survey questions. When the user clicks submit, the server will post this information while also getting the closest related "friend" from the database based on the survey results. The new friend's name and photo will appear for the user!
